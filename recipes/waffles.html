Breakfast Waffles

A waffle is a dish made from leavened batter or dough that is cooked between two plates that are patterned to give a characteristic size, shape, and surface impression

Ingredients

  • 240ml buttermilk
  • 60g unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 large egg
  • 240g plain flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 2/4 tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • oil, for greasing

Steps

  1. Whisk the buttermilk, melted butter, and vanilla togerther in a large bowl
  2. Create a smooth batter then carefully fold through the egg white. Cover with clingfilm and leave the batter to sit for 30 mins
  3. Lightly oil then heat waffle maker. Pour enough batter for one waffle and close the lid, cook waffle until it is golden.
